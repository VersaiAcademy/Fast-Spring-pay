Cancellation Policy

Important Information

This policy outlines the guidelines for canceling transactions conducted through Fast Spring Pay . Please review carefully before requesting cancellation.

Eligibility for Cancellation

You may be eligible to cancel a transaction under the following circumstances:

Change of Plans

If you decide to cancel a transaction due to a change in your plans or circumstances, you may request cancellation subject to the terms and conditions of the transaction.

Error in Transaction

If you notice an error in the transaction details or if the transaction was processed incorrectly, you may request cancellation and correction of the error.

Timing is important: Cancellation requests must be made as soon as possible to be considered.

Cancellation Process

1
Contact Customer Support

Reach out to our support team with your transaction details (ID, date, amount) and reason for cancellation.

2
Review Period

We will review your request and determine eligibility based on transaction terms and conditions.

3
Cancellation Processing

If approved, we will cancel the transaction and initiate any applicable refunds.

4
Confirmation

You will receive confirmation of cancellation and refund details if applicable.

Non-Cancelable Transactions

The following transactions typically cannot be canceled:

  • Transactions that have already been processed and completed
  • Transactions subject to specific terms and conditions or contractual agreements
  • Digital products or services that have been fully delivered
  • Transactions where the merchant has begun providing the service
Note: Some merchants may have their own cancellation policies that supersede Fast Spring Pay 's policy.

Need Help With Cancellation?

Our customer support team is available to assist you with cancellation requests or any questions about our policy.
