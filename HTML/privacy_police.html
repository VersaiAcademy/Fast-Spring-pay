Privacy Policy

General Terms

In order to deliver and enhance our Services, we collect various types of personal information from you, which may include:

Personal details: such as your name, email address, Contact number, payment details and other information when signing up or completing transactions.
Transaction details: includes details about the payouts you send or receive, such as currency, amount and past transactions.
